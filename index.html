Computation Rules (Military Arithmetic)

  • 30-Day Month: Each month is considered to have 30 days.
  • February Adjustment: Feb 28 is computed as 30 days in non-leap years. Feb 29 remains Feb 29.
  • Inclusive Dates: Adding +1 day to differences to include both start and end dates.
  • Year Conversion: For TAFMS years, total days are divided by 360 per personnel standards.
